BARBROS

What it is

  • Responsive, fast and reliable application that connects indepedent Barbers to their clientele.
  • Barbers and clients can (or at some point will be able to): Send messages, set up appointments, request specific services.
  • Implements API from Instagram for barbers to showcase their work. (Someday)
  • Clients and barbers can leave feedback and increase their rep.

What it isn't

  • It's not uber+yelp+tinder for Barbers. Okay, it's kinda like that. But not entirely. It's just different ok?
  • Not for barbershops, but rather for independent barbers looking to attract new clients
  • It isn't gonna sink like the other apps that try this idea. Well, but, we have like almost nothing going atm but there'll be a day where we're #1
  • Not another review app
